FAQ

Questions we get on every first call.

If you have a different one, write to founders@condor.energy — we usually reply within the day.

Where does my meter and contract data actually live?
Encrypted at rest in EU-hosted Postgres (OVH, Gravelines). SOC 2 Type I in progress, Type II by Q4 2026. Your data is never used to train models or shared with other clients.
Do you replace our existing supplier — or sit alongside?
We sit alongside. Condor is supplier-agnostic — we connect to whoever you buy from today. The point is to make you a better customer to them, not to replace them.
What does it cost?
Flat annual fee based on number of sites and GWh under management. Starts at €18K/yr for a single-site industrial; large multi-site portfolios are priced per consumption. The free audit always comes first.
We're a data center / a retailer / not French — does Condor still fit?
Yes for data centers (10 of our 30+ pilots) and retailers (5). Geography: France and Benelux today. Germany and Iberia in pilot for Q3 2026. EU only for now.
How long until I see savings?
Typically your first renewal — most teams capture 8–14% on procurement alone. Hedging discipline compounds from there. If your next renewal is >9 months out, the audit will tell you whether to wait or act now.
